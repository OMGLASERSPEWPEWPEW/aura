The 11 Virtues of Love

We condense the 23 aspects into 11 high-impact spectrums.

I. The Biological Realm (Chemistry)

These are binary. If the Delta is high, the relationship usually dies.

1. Vitality (The Engine)

Spectrum: Restorative ‚Üî High Voltage

Low Score: Loves naps, slow mornings, cozy nights (The "Cat").

High Score: Needs gym, hiking, constant movement, productivity (The "Dog").

The Match: Low Delta Required. A High Voltage person will exhaust a Restorative person.

2. Lust (The Spark)

Spectrum: Reserved ‚Üî Voracious

Low Score: Values quality over quantity, demisexual, touch-sensitive.

High Score: High libido, tactile, needs physical touch to feel safe.

The Match: Low Delta Required. The "Dead Bedroom" predictor.

3. Play (The Child)

Spectrum: Serious/Grounded ‚Üî Absurd/Wild

Low Score: Values dignity, composure, adulthood.

High Score: Values silliness, bits, sarcasm, "goblin mode."

The Match: Medium Delta Allowed. A Serious person often needs a Wild partner to loosen them up (and vice versa).

II. The Emotional Realm (Connection)

These determine how you fight and how you bond.

4. Warmth (The Hearth)

Spectrum: Cool/Stoic ‚Üî Radiant/Emotive

Low Score: Shows love through acts of service, logical support, stability.

High Score: Shows love through words, gushing affirmation, tears.

The Match: Medium Delta is Dangerous. The Radiant person will often feel "starved" by the Stoic person (Anxious/Avoidant trap).

5. Voice (The Sword)

Spectrum: Diplomatic ‚Üî Blunt

Low Score: "Read between the lines," values harmony, hints at needs.

High Score: "Say it to my face," values radical honesty, friction is fine.

The Match: Low Delta Preferred. A Diplomat feels bullied by a Blunt person; a Blunt person feels manipulated by a Diplomat.

6. Space (The Shield)

Spectrum: Merged ‚Üî Autonomous

Low Score: Wants to do everything together. "We."

High Score: Needs a "Man Cave," solo trips, separate bank accounts. "I."

The Match: CRITICAL. If one is High Space and one is Low Space (Merged), this is the Toxic Anxious/Avoidant dynamic.

7. Anchor (The Root)

Spectrum: Fluid/Chaos ‚Üî Structured/Routine

Low Score: Spontaneous, messy, "we'll figure it out," goes with the flow.

High Score: Google Calendar, 5-year plan, clean house, distinct plans.

The Match: Medium Delta is Magic. Structure helps Chaos focus; Chaos helps Structure relax.

III. The Cerebral Realm (Mind)

These determine if you can talk for 40 years.

8. Wit (The Game)

Spectrum: Earnest ‚Üî Intellectual

Low Score: Likes simple, heartfelt, literal conversation.

High Score: Likes banter, debate, layers of irony, dark humor.

The Match: Low Delta Required. A High Wit person will get bored; an Earnest person will feel mocked.

9. Drive (The Mountain)

Spectrum: Content ‚Üî Relentless

Low Score: "Work to live." Values peace, weekends, "enough."

High Score: "Live to work." Values legacy, status, grinding, "more."

The Match: High Friction. The Relentless partner will resent the Content partner's "laziness"; the Content partner will resent being second place to the job.

10. Curiosity (The Horizon)

Spectrum: Traditional ‚Üî Explorer

Low Score: Likes what they like. Nostalgia. Comfort zones.

High Score: Obsessed with the new. Wikipedia rabbit holes. Changing careers.

The Match: Low Delta Preferred. Explorers need other Explorers.

11. Soul (The Meaning)

Spectrum: Pragmatic ‚Üî Idealist

Low Score: Atheist/Realist. "What works?" Material focus.

High Score: Spiritual/Artist. "What does it mean?" Metaphysical focus.

The Match: Flexible. Can work if they respect each other.
